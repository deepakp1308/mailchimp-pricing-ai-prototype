FAQs

  • You can change plans anytime from your Mailchimp account. Open the Account menu, choose Billing, then Plans, select the plan you want, and confirm. Upgrades take effect right away. Downgrades apply at the end of your current billing cycle. Your audiences, templates, and report history move with you.
  • Major credit and debit cards are accepted, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. Customers in eligible regions can also pay with PayPal. For annual plans on larger accounts, invoicing is available. Contact sales to set it up.
  • If your audience grows past your plan's contact tier, your account will be billed at the next tier on your following statement. If you go over your monthly send limit, additional emails are charged at the per-email overage rate listed on your plan. You will see a notice in your dashboard before any overage is applied.
  • Yes. If you regularly send more than 500,000 emails per month or manage a very large list, our Premium plan and custom enterprise tiers include volume pricing, dedicated deliverability support, and SLAs. Talk to sales for a tailored quote.
  1. Pricing shown is in U.S. dollars and reflects monthly billing. Annual billing may include a discount that varies by plan and region. See checkout for the exact rate at your contact tier.
  2. Starting prices apply to the lowest contact tier of each plan. Each plan scales in price as your audience grows. Use the Contacts selector at the top of this page to preview your rate.
  3. Monthly email send limits are calculated as a multiple of your contact tier and reset on your billing date. Sends above your limit are billed at the standard overage rate.
  4. SMS and MMS sends are billed separately from your monthly plan and require an SMS Marketing add-on. Per-message rates vary by destination country and message type.
  5. Generative AI features are subject to fair-use limits. Mailchimp may apply rate limits to prevent abuse. Output is generated and may need review before sending.
  6. Free plan customers receive 30 days of email and chat support after account creation. After that window, in-app help and the knowledge base remain available.
  7. Personalized Onboarding and Dedicated Onboarding Specialist availability depends on plan, region, and contact tier. Talk to sales to confirm eligibility.
  8. Customer outcome figures cited on this page are reported by named customers, used with permission, and reflect their individual results. Your results will vary based on your audience, send strategy, and plan.
